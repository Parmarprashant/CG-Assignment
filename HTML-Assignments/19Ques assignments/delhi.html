DELHI

Delhi, officially known as the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi, is a vibrant and historic metropolis that serves as the political and cultural heart of India. Straddling the Yamuna River, it seamlessly blends ancient heritage with modern dynamism. The city is divided into Old Delhi, with its bustling bazaars and Mughal-era landmarks like the Red Fort and Jama Masjid, and New Delhi, the meticulously planned capital built during British rule, home to India Gate and the Parliament House.
