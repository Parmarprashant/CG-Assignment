MUMBAI

Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra, is the largest city in India and is often called the City of Dreams. It is located on the western coast of India and is the financial, commercial, and entertainment hub of the country. The city never sleeps, as life here is always busy and fast-moving. Mumbai is home to the famous Bollywood film industry, which makes it the heart of Indian cinema. It also houses important financial institutions like the Bombay Stock Exchange and the Reserve Bank of India. The city has many landmarks such as the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves, and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Apart from its modern lifestyle, Mumbai is also known for its cultural diversity. People from all parts of India live here, making it a true melting pot of traditions and languages. Street food like vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri are loved by everyone.
